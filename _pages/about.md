I am Reza Yousefi Maragheh, Staff Data Scientist at [Walmart Labs'](https://www.linkedin.com/company/walmartglobaltech/about/)-Personalization Team. I got My Ph.D. in Operations Research (Data Analytics Track) from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). My Ph.D. thesis was on "Choice Modeling and Recommendation Optimization in Presence of Context Effects" supervised by Professor [Xin Chen](https://ise.illinois.edu/directory/profile/xinchen). My Research interests are Recommender Systems, Information Retrieval, Customer Intent Inference, and Optimization.
